==== Greatly enhanced risk to humans as a consequence of empirically determined lower moist heat stress tolerance ====

https://www.pnas.org/doi/full/10.1073/pnas.2305427120

data availability: 

Original CMIP6 data is freely available for download from the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (https://esgf-node.llnl.gov/search/cmip6/) (77). The downscaled, bias-corrected heat stress dataset is hosted in HPC cluster of Purdue University and be accessed by contacting kong97@purdue.edu. The post-processed data that can be used for reproducing figures in this paper has been deposited by the authors for public use (78).
