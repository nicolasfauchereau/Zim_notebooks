==== Smart Ideas concept note workshop ====

some useful tips: 

* indicate how the research is transformational: transform the way we provide weather and climate data 
* provide sentences to say how you adress the investment signals, e.g. " we will leverage wider investment ....", "we will give effect to VM ..." 
* positioning: 
	* increased demand for actionable information, needs to be probabilistic in nature 
	* align with international efforts to build bridges between the ML and environmental science communities 
	* align with investments in super computing facilities 
	* align with efforts in data rescue (Drew)
