==== StatsForecast ====

**StatsForecast** offers a collection of widely used univariate time series forecasting models, including automatic ARIMA and ETS modeling optimized for high performance using numba. It also includes a large battery of benchmarking models.  

https://nixtla.github.io/statsforecast/ 

see also: 

**mlforecast**: Scalable machine learning based time series forecasting. 

Wrapper for XGBoost and lightGBM with time-series features generation  

→ https://nixtla.github.io/mlforecast/ 

**Neuralforecast**: Scalable and user friendly neural forecasting algorithms for time series data 

→ https://github.com/Nixtla/neuralforecast 

Hierarchical crown forecasting with statistical and econometric methods.
